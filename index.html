How to Make Good Pour-Over Coffee with a Hario V60

By: Jeremy David Rice

Instructions

  1. Heat up your water.
    • Choose the water that you want to make into coffee. Too much or too little mineral content will adversely affect the flavor of your brew. (If your tap water has a chlorinated smell, don't use it.)
    • Heat the water up to 208. The extra heat extracts more flavor from the coffee. Let this beverage cool down for a few minutes before drinking it.
  2. Grind your beans.
  3. Rinse your filter.
  4. Bloom.
  5. Add your brew water.
  6. Drawdown.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
